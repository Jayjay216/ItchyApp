Services

Creating the perfect gift for everyone, backed by blockchain technology!

E-Commerce

We believe the giftcard market is antiquated and not built to serve the mordern consumer, we are creating blockchain driven gift giving ecommerce platform where you can swap giftcards or purcase itchy tokens that are pegged to the UDST(Tether) stable coin to be used at our partnering retailers.

Cross-platform Technology

Built for mobile(Android/IOS) and via chromium browser extensions(Chrome, Edge, Brave, etc..) on the go when you need it via our app on demand. Our cross-platform abilities will create an easy to use and intuitive user experience for our users and incentive them to use our product.

Security

Via the cosmos hub and the solana payment ecosystem, all transactions will be managed and accounted for via the blockchain. Our Dap will live within the Cosmos Ecosystem and use the Sol Wormhole for payment transactions, this will allow for great security and lightning fast transactions.

2022-2023 Road Map

Please check our our road map and stay up to date with our accomplishments. Road map subject to change at any time including but not limited to timelines and events.

  • Itchy Sweater Begins

    March - August 2022

    During this phase we have worked to create our Litepaper, build out our vision and have crafted our Marketing/Customer Hub with read-only access.

  • Purchase/Stake Itchy tokens

    September - December 2022

    During this phase we will allow users to create an Itchy sweater account and purchase itchy tokens via solana by connecting a fantom wallet. We will not yet have a wallet plugin built, so we will not yet distribute Itchy tokens but take proceeds from the sale and hold them for operational use. This will essentially be our kickstarter event. All users that purchase Itchy tokens for staking will receive a 10% reward in Itchy tokens when tokens are distributed. We are limiting this to 10,000 users at a $1000 max. User/Staking ratio will be variable and can fluctuate, with a goal of raising 5 million USD.

  • Itchy App/Wallet Dev Phase

    January - May 2023

    In this phase we will build and release our Itchy app/including our wallet. This wallet will hold Itchy tokens and allow them to be spent at online retailers as well as via a scannable QR code. We are still working on contract architecture and will update this roadmap item when we have decided on a smart contract direction.

  • App Public Debut/Token Airdrop

    June - October

    Once we have built out all prior phases and worked to acquire business partnerships with known retailers, we will airdrop all staked tokens/rewards as well as open up our app/wallet for public use. During this phase we will offer incentives with our partner brands and new staking opportunities within our token ecosytem.
